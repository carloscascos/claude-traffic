Maritime Highway Map - DBSCAN Aggregated Routes

This visualization shows cruise vessel traffic patterns in the Mediterranean using DBSCAN clustering.

About the visualization:

This map demonstrates the use of the DBSCAN (Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise) algorithm to identify and group similar vessel routes into clusters, providing a clearer visualization of maritime traffic patterns.

Line thickness is proportional to the gross tonnage of vessels on each route. Click on any route to see details about the vessels, tonnage, and trip information.

DBSCAN parameters used: eps=0.02, min_samples=2, min_speed=0.2 knots, min_trip_points=5
